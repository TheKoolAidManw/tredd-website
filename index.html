TREDD

Technology Recycling and Eliminating the Digital Divide

A student-run non-profit organization dedicated to eliminating the digital divide by refurbishing unused and unwanted computers, distributing them to children from under-resourced communities.

Our Team

Passionate students making a difference

Student-Led Initiative

TREDD is powered by dedicated students who believe in the power of technology to transform lives. Our team works tirelessly to collect, refurbish, and distribute computers to those in need.
